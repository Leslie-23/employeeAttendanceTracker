Clock In.

QR-based attendance tracking for small shops. Each employee gets a unique QR — scan it, tap a button, they're clocked in. The server verifies they're physically at the shop with WiFi + GPS. No hardware, no subscriptions, no paper sheets.

// 01

QR Identity

Every employee gets a unique, unguessable QR. Print it, tape it to their desk, or let them save it to their phone — it's theirs alone.

// 02

Dual Verify

Every clock-in checks either the shop's WiFi subnet or GPS proximity (within 100m). Can't fake your way in from home.

// 03

Live Analytics

Daily clock-in counts, hours worked per employee, CSV backfill, and a full audit log — all in one admin page.

How It Works

// the 4-step employee flow
01
Scan
Employee scans their personal QR on their phone.
02

Their clock page opens. One button: Clock In or Clock Out.
03

Server checks WiFi or GPS. Rejects anything off-site.
04
Done
Record saved with timestamp, IP, coords, and method.
